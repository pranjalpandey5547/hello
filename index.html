About FoodShare AI

We Believe No Food Should Be Wasted

FoodShare AI is India's smartest food waste management platform, connecting surplus food from restaurants, events, and households to NGOs, volunteers, and animal shelters using artificial intelligence and real-time location services.

🎯

Our Mission

Eliminate preventable food waste in India by creating a seamless bridge between food surplus and food need.

🤖

AI-Powered

Our AI analyzes food quantity, expiry times, and local demand to recommend the fastest and most impactful redistribution.

🌱

Sustainable

Every meal redirected from landfill reduces methane emissions and supports sustainable food systems across India.
